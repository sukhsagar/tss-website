About This Event

IRIS is being organized to celebrate the 17th birth anniversary of Technical Students' Society,
which was founded back in 2003 by the students of Deptt. of Computer Science
GNDU, Amritsar

Join us for three days of fun, laughter, entertainment, interactive events and competitions, and amazing ambeince that will keep you looking ahead.

FAQ

Here is a list of answers to frequently asked questions about the summer fest.

  • 01. General FAQ
  • 02. Registration
Where will all these events be hosted?

All the above mentioned events will be hosted in the campus premises of GURU NANAK DEV UNIVERSITY, Amritsar.

I am not a student of GNDU. Can I still participate?

Yes. The event is not limited to the students of any particular university. Any student can participate. Just bring you ID card along with you as a proof.

I study in school. In which events can I participate?

Yes. We have a few events for you as well. You can participate in Open Mic, Drink and Code, Web Development, GGT, Mr and Miss IRIS, Ad Mad, 0-Budget, and all the sports events. You can also attend the two workshops.

Can I participate in more than one event?

Yes. You can participate in multiple events. But, please be careful about their timings.

Can I register online?

You can register online by sending a message on WhatsApp. But, your ticket will be issued once you pay the money at the registration counter set up outside CS Deptt. GNDU

Can we get a discount for group registrations?

If you want to register in a group of 10 or more, we have some exciting discounts for you. You can drop a text at 9646421291.
