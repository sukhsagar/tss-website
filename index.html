Welcome to TSS.

We are a community of future leaders who are experts in their own Dimensions. We in TSS help and guide students . Be it a problem of a small magnitude to a large one, Someone is always there to help.

Whether you want to be part of a specific club or you are having problems with the syllabus,TSS is the answer.

Our members are the seniors, students like you, so they can help you understand the ways of Computer Science Department and Guru Nanak Dev University itself.

Upskilling

The main Goal of the organisation is to update the skills of the student. Various measures are taken and various events are organised for this very purpose.

Round- the- clock Availability

The students can contact the members of society at any time. Someone is always there to help.

Representatives

We are the departments's representative at the university level. So teams are formed for various events and students are given chances to showcase their Spark time and again.

Recreation

As it is well known that
"All work and no play,makes jack a dull boy"
So to keep the students lively and on-their-toes,various fun events are organised.

Synergy

Being a part of tss,students get to learn how to channelize their energies to achieve a common goal.

Cool Staff

The members of TSS are also students of our own department. The members are very friendly so that there is no awkwardness or hesitation.

WHAT WE DO?

We are a community that focuses on not one because multiple things, as it is essential to be a multi-tasker in modern fast-moving IT  industry. So the fields we try to underline come under the category of technical and non technical.

Technical Events and Flash Classes.

 Talent without ample opportunities goes waste. So to recognize budding talent and to bring about exposure various technical events are held. 
Along with these flash classes are arranged to teach students various subjects,at a very nominal registration fee.

Workshops and Seminars

As we are to be professionals in IT industry which changes every ,say 6 months,so we need to keep ourselves up-to-date with the latest technologies. Keeping in mind,this very purpose
different workshops are conducted from time to time

Fun events

Along with all the technical stuff,loads of fun  events are also arranged, be it festival celebrations or other competitions.
TSS organises these so that the student don't get bored and also to awaken the team spirit among students  which is helpful in long term.
